# Privacy Policy — Musical Vibe Check

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

SkyLabs Systems ("we", "our", "us") built Musical Vibe Check as a free app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected
- **Anonymous User ID** — We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a temporary, anonymous identifier for your device. This is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
- **Game Data** — Room codes, display names you choose, song selections, and votes are stored temporarily in Firebase Realtime Database to enable gameplay.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Email addresses or real names
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Health or financial data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Analytics or tracking data

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used **solely** to:
- Enable real-time multiplayer gameplay
- Maintain active game rooms
- Display scores and results to players in the same room

## Data Retention

- Game room data is temporary and associated with a single game session.
- We do not retain personal data after a game session ends.
- Anonymous user IDs are managed by Firebase and are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| **Firebase** (Google) | Anonymous auth & real-time game state | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| **iTunes Search API** (Apple) | Song search & 30-second previews | [Apple Privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy

Musical Vibe Check does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication only.

## Security

Game data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). We use Firebase's built-in security infrastructure to protect data in transit and at rest.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](../../issues) on this repository.
